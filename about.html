About us

Welcome to TOROS

Toros, founded in 2005, is a renowned footwear manufacturer based in India with over 19 years of industry experience. Specializing in the production of PU footwear, our focus primarily lies in manufacturing ladies and gents sandals. With an impressive portfolio, we offer over 150 designs for ladies and 200 designs for gents, ensuring a diverse range of options to cater to various tastes and preferences. Our production unit, Roseindfootcare company, exemplifies our commitment to quality and innovation. Equipped with advanced technology and operated by skilled professionals, we ensure the highest standards of craftsmanship in every pair of sandals we produce.

Our dedication to excellence has enabled us to expand our reach beyond India. We proudly export our footwear to countries such as the UAE, Bahrain, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Oman, and India & East Africa . By offering superior comfort, trendy designs, and reliable durability, Toros has established a strong presence in international markets. We understand the importance of footwear in enhancing individual style and confidence, and it is our passion to provide customers around the world with exceptional products that exceed their expectations. With a commitment to customer satisfaction and a constant drive for innovation, Toros continues to make strides in the global footwear industry.
