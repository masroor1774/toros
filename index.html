About us

Welcome to Toros

Toros, founded in 2005, is a renowned footwear manufacturer based in India with over 19 years of industry experience. Specializing in the production of PU footwear, our focus primarily lies in manufacturing ladies and gents sandals. With an impressive portfolio, we offer over 150 designs for ladies and 200 designs for gents, ensuring a diverse range of options to cater to various tastes and preferences. Our production unit, Roseindfootcare company, exemplifies our commitment to quality and innovation. Equipped with advanced technology and operated by skilled professionals, we ensure the highest standards of craftsmanship in every pair of sandals we produce.
